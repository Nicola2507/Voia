Interlaken sits in a green valley between two vivid blue lakes, with the
snow-capped Jungfrau massif rising behind — the launchpad for the big-mountain
Swiss Alps. This is the adrenaline end of the catalog: paragliding off the
hillsides, scenic rail up to glacier country, and the fairy-tale waterfalls of
the Lauterbrunnen valley next door. Come in summer for hiking and flying, or
in winter for the snow. It's drama on a grand scale. *(No packaged trip yet —
explore this one your way.)*
