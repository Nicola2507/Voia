Bali is the flagship "warm in winter" escape — a lush Indonesian island of
emerald rice terraces, cliffside temples, and volcano silhouettes at dawn. It
flexes to whatever you're after: surf and beach clubs on the coast, yoga and
slow mornings in Ubud, waterfalls and rice-paddy walks inland. When it's grey
and cold back home, this is the daydream. Give it a full week or more — it's a
long way to go for a weekend.
