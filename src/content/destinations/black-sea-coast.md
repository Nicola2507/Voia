Mamaia is Romania's headline beach resort — a slim sandbar of golden sand
between the sea and a lake, lined with hotels, terraces, and a summer-long
buzz. Next door, Constanța is one of the country's oldest cities, where you
can trade the beach for a wander past the restored seafront Casino and traces
of ancient Tomis. It's the easy, cheerful choice for a sun-and-sea break
without leaving the country — best in high summer, when the whole coast comes
alive.
